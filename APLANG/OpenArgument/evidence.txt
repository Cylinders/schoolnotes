First, Second, and Third S: 

	Subjects: 

		Think about all the subjects that you take in school.

		class subjects in school. 


	Society: 

		Things learned from media, sports, religion, news, etc.

	Self: 

		You can and are allowed to use examples from own or family or
		known peoples' experiences. 

		The weakest examples of evidence come from this part.
